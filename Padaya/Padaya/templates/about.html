About Pâ dayâ

Pâ dayâ is a platform dedicated to connecting Liberian farmers directly with markets. Our mission is to strengthen the agricultural value chain, empower local farmers, and make fresh, affordable products accessible to everyone.

By cutting out the middlemen, we ensure fair prices for farmers and affordable quality for buyers. Together, we are building a stronger, self-sufficient Liberia.
